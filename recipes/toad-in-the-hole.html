Toad in the Hole

Description

An english classic of sausages cooked in simple batter.

Perfect comfort food for those looking for a simple dish for the family.

    For the batter

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 150mL semi-skimmed milk

    For the toad

  • 8 pork sausages
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
